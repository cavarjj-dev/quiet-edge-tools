Script Your Cues

For each situation, write one cue word (1–3 words max). Then run it through the 4-Rule checklist. A cue that fails any rule needs revision before testing.

The 4 Rules
① Short — 1–3 words (1 word is ideal)   ② Action-focused — what TO do, not what to avoid   ③ Feel-based — evokes the physical sensation of correct execution   ④ Tested — in practice BEFORE competition

Add situations in Step 1 to begin scripting cues.

3

7-Day Testing Log

Test each cue in practice every day this week. A cue is not competition-ready until it feels automatic. This is Rule 4 in action.

Script cues in Step 2 to unlock your testing log.

Your Pocket Cue Card

Generate a printable cue card once your cues are tested and ready. Laminate it and keep it with your gear as a pre-competition reference.
