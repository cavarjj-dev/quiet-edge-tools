Start Smaller Than You Think

Your minimum viable session (MVS) is what you commit to even on your worst day — exhausted, busy, low energy. If you do only this, the day still counts.

Why this works: A small system you follow 7/7 beats a big system you follow 3/7. The MVS protects your streak on hard days — and most days you'll exceed it once you've started.
