Pre-Competition Environment Audit

Am I supporting or disrupting my athlete's pre-game routine? Score your behaviors, see your pattern, get one focused action.
Before you begin: Bring a specific recent competition day to mind — not your best day, but a typical one. Think about the 2 hours before competition: the car ride, arrival, and warm-up period. Answer based on what you actually do, not what you intend to do.

Research shows that a parent's emotional state is transmitted to their athlete through tone, pacing, and body language — even without words. Your pre-competition behavior is part of your athlete's preparation environment, whether you realize it or not.
