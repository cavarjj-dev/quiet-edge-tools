The Science Behind This

Your brain wires what you repeat — not what you intend. Every rep you execute after quality drops is practicing the fatigued, sloppy version of the skill.


Ericsson's deliberate practice research shows expertise builds in focused, quality bursts (typically 60–90 min max) — not through grinding volume. Elite athletes don't train longer. They protect quality obsessively.
