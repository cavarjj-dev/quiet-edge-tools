Your Practice Design
The 20–30% Rule

You don't need to make every practice exhausting. The research says 20–30% of your total practice time in competition conditions is enough to close the gap.

One session per week with deliberate competition conditions changes what competition feels like. Over time, pressure stops feeling foreign — it just feels like another day of training.
